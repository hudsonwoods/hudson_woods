Where Designssss
Meets Nature

Set amidst forests and meadows with sweeping mountain views, Hudson Woods homes offer modern design that blends seamlessly with the natural surroundings.

In the midst of the Hudson River Valley and just two hours from New York City, Hudson Woods is an experience that opens doors to discovering the things that matter. Family. Friends. Sport. Craft. Hudson Woods lets you access it all.

Encompassing 131 acres, Hudson Woods features 26 architect-designed dwellings meticulously crafted from locally-sourced, sustainable materials.
